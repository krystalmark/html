Delicious Lasagna Recipe

By: Krystal Mark

  • Ingredients
  • Instructions
  • Tips

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can (28 ounces) crushed tomatoes
  • 1 can (6 ounces) tomato paste
  • 1 can (6 ounces) tomato sauce
  • 2 teaspoons sugar
  • 2 teaspoons dried basil leaves
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 2 tablespoons fresh parsley, chopped
  • 6 lasagna noodles, cooked and drained
  • 2 cups ricotta cheese
  • 1 egg, beaten
  • 3 cups mozzarella cheese, shredded
  • 3/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese

Instructions

  1. In a large skillet, cook the ground beef over medium heat until browned. Drain excess fat.
  2. Add chopped onion and minced garlic to the skillet. Cook until onion is translucent.
  3. Stir in crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, tomato sauce, sugar, basil, salt, and pepper. Simmer for 30 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  4. In a separate bowl, mix together ricotta cheese and beaten egg.
  5. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  6. In a 9x13 inch baking dish, layer lasagna noodles, ricotta mixture, mozzarella cheese, and meat sauce.
  7. Repeat layers, ending with a layer of meat sauce on top.
  8. Sprinkle grated Parmesan cheese on top.
  9. Bake in preheated oven for 25-30 minutes, or until bubbly and golden.

Tips

  • Let the lasagna rest for 10 minutes before slicing to help it hold its shape.
  • Feel free to add your favorite vegetables to the sauce for extra flavor.
  • For a vegetarian option, substitute the ground beef with your favorite plant-based protein.
